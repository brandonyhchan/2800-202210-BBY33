Mission Statement

At hope in a box, we are trying to create a simplified, personal way to donate money to people around the world experiencing hardships. Our goal is to have the user know exactly how their donations contribute to people in need, by purchasing packages containing essential items such as food, water and clothing.

Our Team

Hope in a Box was founded in 2022 by 4 Term 1 CST students at BCIT for COMP 2800. We are based out of Metro Vancouver, BC.
