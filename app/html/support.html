Support

Welcome to Hope in a Box's support page! Here you can find step by step guides to help you choose packages to send, checkout using a credit card, and more! For more specific inquiries or issues, please email support@hopeinabox.com, and will try to get back to you as soon as possible. If you are a charity and are interested in working with us, please email partnerships@hopeinabox.com, and we will gladly set up a meeting with your organization!

Creating an account

You will need an account to use any of Hope in a Box's services.

  1. Click on the "create account" button on the main page.
  2. Fill in your first and last name, your email address, create a unique username, and choose a new password.
  3. Click on the "sign up" button.
  4. That's it! Now you can sign in and start sending packages!

Changing your profile photo

  1. Login to your account.
  2. Click on the "Profile" tab found in the header on desktop, or tap on the "Profile" icon in the footer on mobile.
  3. Click/tap on the "Choose files" button, and upload a profile picture from your device.
  4. Click/tap on the "Change photo" button
  5. Refresh the page, you should now see your updated profile photo!

Changing your username

  1. Login to your account.
  2. Click on the "Profile" tab found in the header on desktop, or tap on the "Profile" icon in the footer on mobile.
  3. Click/tap on your username, and type in your new username.
  4. Press "Enter", and click/tap on the "Update account" button in the popup.
  5. Now your username has been updated!

Updating your email address

  1. Login to your account.
  2. Click on the "Profile" tab found in the header on desktop, or tap on the "Profile" icon in the footer on mobile.
  3. Click/tap on your email, and type in the updated email.
  4. Press "Enter", and click/tap on the "Update account" button in the popup.
  5. Now your email has been updated!

Changing your password

  1. Login to your account.
  2. Click on the "Profile" tab found in the header on desktop, or tap on the "Profile" icon in the footer on mobile.
  3. Click/tap on the "Current password" field, and type in your current password.
  4. Click/tap on the "Change password" field, and type in your new password.
  5. Click/tap on the "Change Password" button, and then click/tap on the "Update account" button in the popup.
  6. Now your password has been updated!

Purchasing a Package

  1. Login to your account.
  2. Click on the "Map" tab found in the header on desktop, or tap on the "Map" icon in the footer on mobile.
  3. The countries in red are where you can currently choose to send packages. Click/tap on any red country.
  4. The available packages will be displayed on the left side on desktop, or above the map on mobile. Click/tap on the "Add" button to add a package to your cart. You can click/tap on the "More info" button to see specific details about the package.
  5. To see items in your cart, click/tap on the "Cart" icon in the top right corner of the navbar.
  6. The cart menu is where you can fine tune the quantities, remove packages, clear your cart, see the total transaction price, and checkout.
  7. To checkout, click/tap on the "Checkout" button, which will redirect you to the payment page.
  8. Enter your credit cart number, expiry date and security code, and click/tap on "Purchase".
  9. You have now purchased a package!

Viewing Order History

  1. Login to your account.
  2. Click on the "Orders" tab found in the header on desktop, or tap on the "Profile" icon in the footer in mobile.
  3. Here you can view all the previous packages that have been ordered!
