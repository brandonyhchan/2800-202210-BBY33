How It Works

1) Select a Country in Need of Aid
2) Add Care Packages to Cart
3) Checkout Cart Items
4) Care Package Delivered to Recipient

Hope in a Box allows users to purchase and send care packages to a location in need of aid. Upon checkout, users may attach a personalized message to the recipient. In doing so, the user has a higher degree of control over their donations, as well as a closer connection to the recipient compared to donating through a traditional charity. Hope in a Box partners with local non-profit organizations to ensure that your care packages reach the intended targets. For more information, please visit our "Who We Are" and "Partnerships" pages.
