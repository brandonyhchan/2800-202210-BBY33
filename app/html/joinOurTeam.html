Join our Team

Here at Hope in a Box, we are always looking for talented individuals to help us send out more packages to those in need. For more information on about Hope in a Box and our team, visit the "Who We Are" page.

Benefits- What do we bring to the table?

Flexible work hours- Not a morning person? No problem! Need to see the doctor or dentist? Make that appointment. Our office does not have a set schedule, as we value the quality of work and understand people can be more productive at different times during the day.

Remote Work- Ever since the pandemic, work culture has changed, and our company has decided to change with it. We offer our staff the flexibility to work from home if they choose, and are only required to be in the office for certain meetings.

Meaningful Work- At Hope in a Box, we want to help as many people as we can, and make a difference for those in need. Everyday your work contributes to getting more donations and sending out more packages.

Professional & Personal Development- Being at the cutting edge of tech, our staff is constantly learning and applying new skills. We will support you throughout this process in any way we can.
